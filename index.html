Hi! I'm a first year PhD student at UCLA with research interests in labor and public economics, particularly in early life policies, education, and inequality.

Before this, I was at UBC working on projects related to crime with Sam Norris, industrial policy with Reka Juhasz, and apprenticeships with Jamie McCasland.

I hold a B.A. in Economics from McGill University.
